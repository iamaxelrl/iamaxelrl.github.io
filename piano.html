Piano

The piano has always been, for me, a way to express my emotions through music and to share them with others. But playing the piano also requires discipline, commitment, and determination: working for hours, for weeks on a single piece, practicing again and again until I succeed, sometimes on just one measure, and never giving up!

Taking part in competitions, with a heightened sense of rivalry, also means stepping out of my comfort zone and sometimes performing in front of hundreds of people.

For me, the piano represents a great challenge, an opportunity that allows me to grow not only technically and musically, but also on a personal level.
