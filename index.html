About Me

I studied for two years with CNED to prepare my Baccalauréat independently. This was extremely rewarding, as it allowed me to develop strong discipline and a rigorous sense of organization.

I am currently studying at IDRAC Business School in Toulouse. In this school, I was able to develop important skills that apply to all fields, such as public speaking, teamwork, project management, as well as immersion in the business world. I initially chose a business program because I was not sure about my orientation.

What I know today is that it helped me a lot, as I was able to acquire both soft skills and hard skills, and to clarify my goals. Today, my goals are clearer: I need technical challenges. I want to see my studies and my life as a challenge. That is why I now wish to move towards an engineering school, to pursue a demanding program based on rigor, creativity and innovation, and problem-solving.
