<h2> Computer Sciences </h2>

Since my early age, I have been passionate about computer science. I have always liked exploring what is hidden behind systems. I started by installing and testing different operating systems, whether on my computer (notably Linux) or through virtual machines. I became interested in creating networks and domains, deploying applications thanks to Windows Server and Windows RemoteApp. I also enjoyed recreating domains, configuring sessions to imitate my middle school’s network. It was a way for me to learn through practice and experimentation.

I created Windows applications in C# and C++ thanks to YouTube tutorials. I am also interested in web development through HTML / CSS. I also created a chat application in PHP. For this project, I became interested in databases, especially to manage the tables for users and messages. I also experimented with installing a video CMS portal (similar to YouTube) on Linux 20, in order to host my own video sharing platform.

More recently, I have become interested in Python because it allows me to automate tasks and to explore fields that fascinate me such as AI, cybersecurity, and data analysis.

I love learning, I like tinkering, testing, and creating. I need a demanding and stimulating environment to progress. This is what today drives me to pursue engineering studies, particularly in computer science, since I have been interested in it from my early age.
